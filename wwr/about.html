History

Founded in 2010, our rafting company began with a passion for adventure and a love for the outdoors. What started as a small group of guides offering river tours has grown into one of the most trusted rafting experiences in the region. Over the years, we have taken thousands of guests on unforgettable journeys down scenic rivers, always prioritizing safety, fun, and a deep respect for nature.

In 2012, whitewater rafting started gaining popularity as a recreational sport. Commercial rafting companies began to appear, offering guided trips through some of the most exciting river rapids in the world. Over time, improved raft designs, better safety gear, and increased interest in outdoor adventure made rafting a popular activity for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike.
